Background

Recently swarm robotics has been gathering attention because swarm robots are more flexible and autonomous than single robots in many cases. The movement of swarm robots refer to the group behavior of ants and fish and so on. For example, the research group in Harvard University is trying to develop ‘thousand-robot swarm'. In this system, more than 1000 robots form a programmed figure autonomously.
Swarm robots can be useful in many fields, and one of the fields is the body. By administering the robots in the body, many kinds of data will be taken, and swarm robots have the possibility of assisting fertilization and deliverying the drug. However, it is necessary to make nano-scale robots for the application in the body, and it is difficult for electric robots. Then we suggest swarm robots made of biomolecules.
In previous research, the convenience of the molecular sensing, using the aggregation of the gold nano particle whose surface is modified with DNA strands, was indicated. By using this technology, higher sensitivity is realized. In our project, swarm robots ware made by beads and DNA strands, and the more practical swarm movement was set as the object of research.

Objective

In conventional DNA amplification, high temperature cycle is needed. However, high temperature cycle is unsuitable in vivo and high temperature affects to many kinds of materials in samples such like proteins. Then the objective of our project is the swarm robot system which amplify target DNAs and detect them under isothermal process.
The materials of the swarm robot are DNA strands and the magnetic beads covered with DNA. When target DNAs are in surrounding environment, they are captured by primers in the solution. Primer amplifies the target DNA and nicking enzyme cuts out the target DNA from the primer, then target DNA is released into the solution. By keeping repeating this processing, target DNAs exponentially increase. One of the novelties of this method is the relationship of target DNAs and primers. In general, target DNA sequence is the template and primers bond to the specific sequence in target DNAs. On the other hand, primer becomes template in our project.
By using this method, we can detect extremely low concentration sequences keeping isothermal environment. This method is useful for application of detection in vivo.
