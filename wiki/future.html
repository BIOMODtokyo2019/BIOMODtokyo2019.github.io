Future

1. In this research, some patterns of logic gate systems were created. If we realize some other kinds of logic gate systems such as XOR gate and XNOR gate, more complicated patterns about 2 signals of X and Y can be detected. Generally, when the specific materials in vivo are detected with simple method, only the existence is brought as a result in many cases. Especially it’s difficult to detect low concentration. On the other hand, our logic gate has possibility of detecting large range of concentration of multiple materials, because it can change the state of the aggregation along with the surrounding environment. For example, if nano-scale swarm robots can deliver some drug, more accurate administration according to the concentration of the material become possible.

2. Recently, eDNA is focused on as a index of the soil and water environment. In previous research, the pollutant resolution of soil microorganisms was analyzed with plasmid. Like this research, our nano-scale swarm robots are useful for short ow concentration DNA fragments because of its high sensitivity. For example, when some phenomenon is occurred by two genes, the degree of contribution of two kinds of genes and the degree of the progress of the phenomenon can be measured by using logic gates.
3. In the future, beads can make special structure like photonic crystal. Photonic crystal is the structure composed of periodically lined up materials, and each material has different refractive index. If the aggregation of beads can be controlled in the solution by our swarm robot, we can intentionally change the local concentration of beads. This technology enables us to guide light to the direction we think without large loss of light.
